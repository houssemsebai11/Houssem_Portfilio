Album Cover Design

This project showcases the comprehensive Visual Identity and Promotional Design for the R&B/Trap EP, "HUSTLER," by the artist. The design utilizes a high-contrast black and white aesthetic infused with street culture motifs, including floating cash and a distinctive Gothic font. Deliverables include the main Album Art/Mockup featuring the signature black "Hustler" box, a set of five unique promotional track posters for Spotify/YouTube, optimized streaming visuals, and a separate, striking vectorial style portrait of the artist, Spido, ensuring a cohesive and high-impact release campaign.
